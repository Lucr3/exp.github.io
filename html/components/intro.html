Il Conflitto nello Yemen

In an ever-growing, increasingly connected world, where everything is shared and visible to everyone, there are countries experiencing dramatic situations but which, despite being in full view of the whole world, remain unseen.
Countries where war, hunger, thirst, and pain are the order of the day.
Countries where a child's hope is not to receive a toy, but to survive another day.
Countries whose voices are drowned out by the noise of the world, but which are there and continue to resist despite everything.
It is to those countries that we want to give back a voice, it is to those countries that we want to restore hope.
This is not the story of a single country, but the story of thousands of countries, people, children, and mothers.
This is the story of Yemen.
