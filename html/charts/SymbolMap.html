A decade of suffering transformed into a living history. With this interactive geospatial visualization, explore not only where the conflict has struck, but how the war fronts have shifted and intensified from 2014 to 2025.
Look at each “hot spot” on the map. It is not just a coordinate: it is the accumulation of numerous violent events, each weighted by the human cost it entails. Variable density markers do something extraordinary: they instantly reveal critical areas, showing how the pressure of war has shifted over time, saturating entire regions while others have remained relatively intact.
