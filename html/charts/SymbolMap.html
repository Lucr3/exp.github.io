The Geography of Conflict
The geographical evolution of the conflict and the human toll, year after year
1.0s
—

This geospatial visualization transforms a decade of record casualties into a dynamic narrative. Through an interactive timeline, it's possible to observe not only where the conflict struck, but how the war fronts migrated and intensified from 2014 to 2025.
Each "hotspot" on the map represents not a simple geographic coordinate, but an accumulation of violent events weighted by their human cost. The use of variable-density markers allows critical areas to be identified at a glance, revealing how the pressure of war has shifted over time, saturating entire regions while leaving others relatively untouched.
