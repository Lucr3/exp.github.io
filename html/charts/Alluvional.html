Water, Viruses, and Storms: The Parallel Crisis
Hierarchical breakdown of disaster events by type and specific impact

As battlefronts shift, Yemen wages a silent war against nature and disease. This flood diagram breaks down the collateral crises that have devastated the country over the past decade.
The most significant data streams reveal two dramatic truths: the extreme fragility of the territory in the face of flash floods and the collapse of the healthcare system, which has paved the way for massive outbreaks of cholera and viral diseases. The visualization traces the hierarchical path from a general disaster category to the specific triggering event.
